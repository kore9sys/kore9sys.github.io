Our mission

kore9 is a provider of embedded hardware and software services. It enables OEMs and service providers to wirelessly monitor, control, and maintain distributed networks of products and devices. kore9 offers a hosted solution which is readily scalable, secure, and includes full data analytics and reporting capabilities.

NODZ™ Platform

The kore9 NODZ™ Platform is a graphical environment that supports Application and IOT Nodes. By simplifying the way that nodes are connected we are able to facilitate the connection of virtually any application or IOT endpoint.

Both Many to One and One to Many connections are possible, and our platform excels in the connection of many seemingly disparate applications and devices. NODZ™ utilizes a robust cloud-based architecture that is scalable and high-performance.

Hardware

While Kore9 Systems is a software company, we occasionally have need for custom hardware solutions to offer our customers. When we initially went looking for customizable, flexible, off-the-shelf hardware to offer our customers, we didn't see much.

So we developed a core module to support a variety of sensors and custom endpoints. This module, and the recent proliferation of OEM wifi modules, have opened the door to a host of industrial and agricultural markets that we wouldn't have otherwise had access to. Call us to discuss your custom hardware application!


C1 Module

Version 1 kore9 module - this is the board that started it all, at a time when single board systems were a rarity. This board is a precursor to the mini, micro, nano, and long range (with antenna). It is included here for completeness and not recommended for new applications.


V2 Standard

Our latest module integrates wifi a,b,g,n with fully featured on-board microprocessor capable of integrating with virtually any sensor or actuator. See the sensors and actuators sections below for some ideas!


Mini

The mini core module sports limited i/o points to offer compact and custom board-level solutions such as wifi buttons, compact sensors, and integrations into existing enclosurs. Contact us with your specifications for compact IOT applications!
