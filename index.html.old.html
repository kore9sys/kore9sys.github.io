Our mission

kore9 is a provider of embedded hardware and software services. It enables OEMs and service providers to wirelessly monitor, control, and maintain distributed networks of products and devices. kore9 offers a hosted solution which is readily scalable, secure, and includes full data analytics and reporting capabilities.

NODZ™ Platform

The kore9 NODZ™ Platform is a graphical environment to support the connection of Application and IOT Nodes. By simplifying the way that nodes are connected we can facilitate the connection of virtually any application or IOT endpoint. Both Many to One and One to Many connections are possible, and our platform excels in the connection of many seemingly disparate applications and devices. NODZ™ utilizes a robust cloud-based architecture that is scalable and high-performance.
